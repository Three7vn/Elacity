Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is Elacity? +

Elacity is a real-time AI browser extension that helps you read academic papers faster and more intelligently. It parses content, extracts key insights, and provides contextual analysis as you browse research papers.

How does Elacity work? +

Elacity activates automatically when you visit academic papers, arXiv preprints, or scientific articles. It uses AI to parse the content in real-time, highlighting key findings and providing instant insights to accelerate your reading comprehension.

What types of documents does Elacity support? +

Elacity currently works with arXiv papers and preprints, with plans to expand support to other academic platforms and scientific articles. It seamlessly integrates with your browser to provide real-time analysis of research content.
